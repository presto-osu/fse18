Description

Get the greatest watchfaces for your Android Wear watch. Watch Faces For Android Wear is a collection of the best designs you will find for your watch. Activate watchfaces with a simple touch on your smartphone. New watchfaces are provided continuously. Please note: You have to choose "Watchface Designs" as your watchface on watch.


Discover more about Android Wear with "Android Wear Center": http://tinyurl.com/m87stxs


Features:
Big collection of free watchfaces
Analog and digital watchfaces
Free watchfaces
More than 40 Watchfaces by designers
Compatible with all Android Wear devices
Biggest collection of awesome designs
Simple one touch activation
New watchfaces are added continously


You have to own an Android Wear device to use this app
Currently compatible devices:
HUAWEI Watch
Moto 360 Gen 2 (2015)
Moto 360 Gen 1 (2014)
Asus Zenwatch
LG G Watch
LG G Watch R
LG G Watch Urbane (Android Wear version)
Samsung Gear Live
Sony SmartWatch 3


Please note:
-Make sure your mobile is compatible with Android Wear.
-Make sure to pair your phone with the wearable device with help of the Android Wear App.
-You have to choose Watchface Designs as your Watchface on watch. After that choose watchface from phone app
