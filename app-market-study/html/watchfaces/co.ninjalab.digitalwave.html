Description

Digital Wave is an Android Wear watch face that makes simplicity its best feature.

It takes full inspiration from Sony Smartwatch 3 default face, but we have improved it making it easier to use, with better animations, with changeable colors and making it compatible with round watches.

The seconds ticks are animated with a wave effect while the colors of the minutes digits are beautifully faded.

It is totally configurable and you can change background colors both in ambient and normal modes, hours, minutes and seconds. You can change fonts and their sizes.

Fully compatible with all Android Wear devices (Moto 360, Lg Wear, Samsung, Sony, Asus,...)
