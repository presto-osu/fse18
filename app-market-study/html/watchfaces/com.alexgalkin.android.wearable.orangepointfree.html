Description

Orange Point Watch Face is a stylish and useful analog watch face especially designed for every day use.
Works on Moto 360, LG G Watch R, Sony Smartwatch 3, Samsung Gear Live, Asus ZenWatch and otherAndroid Wear devices.
Supports round and square displays.

Key features:

- Date and day of the week.
- Watch battery digital and graphical indicator.
- Customizable color of the clock hands and battery indicator.

Find more great themes and features in the premium version http://goo.gl/CgsyMn

Get additional info about our watch faces at:
Google+: https://goo.gl/xvm05b
Twitter: https://twitter.com/watchstudio1
Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/groups/482369191929905/

To install:
1. After install run 'Resync app' on Android Wear App.
2. Long press your android wear watch and choose Orange Point Free Watch Face as you watch face.
