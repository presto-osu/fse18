Description

Golden Beauty is a timeless masterpiece for your Android Wear device. This watch face design turns your Moto 360 or any other smartwatch into a high-end timepiece.

The Tourbillon, the gears which can be activated in the settings of the smartphone app, integrates beautifully with the luxury watch face.

You can also customize various colors of your watch face (premium feature) or hide the hour indicators for a pure golden experience



More exclusive watchfaces:

Visit our exclusive wearface collection for the Moto 360 on http://goo.gl/QOsOu4. Also please let us know if you like to have any other watch faces to be added to our selection of watch faces.
