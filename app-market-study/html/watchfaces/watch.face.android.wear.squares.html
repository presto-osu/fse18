Description

Classic Watch Face turns your Android Wear device into an exceptional classic timepiece.

Designed in the pursuit of clarity, beauty and precision, Classic watch face is one of the most iconic watch faces for your Android Wear device.

Classic watchface is optimized for circular smartwatches such as the Moto 360 and rectangular watches as the Samsung Gear Live and the LG G Watch. Classic follows the digital watchmaker tradition.

For more custom watch faces please visit our watchface collection on http://goo.gl/QOsOu4

----
How to install Classic Watch Face:

- Download the free app on Google Play
- Open it on your mobile
- Tap & hold on the watch face on the Android Wear device
- Select one of watch faces

In case the watch face is not present, please go to your Android Wear app on the smartphone and in settings press on synchronize apps. Then you can become your own watchmaker.
----

Visit our exclusive wearface collection for the Moto 360 and other Android Wear watches on the Play Store http://goo.gl/QOsOu4. Also please let us know if you like to have any other watch faces to be produced by our watchmakers and to be added to our selection.
