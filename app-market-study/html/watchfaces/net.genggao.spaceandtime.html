Description

I originally designed the artwork for the Moto 360 Design Face-off contest. Since then I have been asked to make a functional watch face by many people.

The wait is finally over, this watch face is build with the official Android Wear Watch Face API. I hope you enjoy this fun watch face.

Features:
Support both round and square watch
Support ambient mode
24-hour and 12-hour Settings in Android Wear companion app

More customization options will be added in future updates.
