Description

BOOST! is the collection of fine-crafted watch faces specially made for Android Wear smartwatches. Choose from 30+ of exclusive animated and interactive watch faces - both free and premium designs.

All of our watch faces are perfectly optimized (ensuring low energy consumption) and constantly updated with new features, making sure that you always have the best right at your fingertips. Join the community of 10.000+ fans try, customize and win watch faces.

Main features:
All our exclusive, fine-crafted watch faces at one place;
Preview and try designs before installing;
10+ free watch faces available;
Tweak and personalize options, features & ambient modes;
All modern functional complications including:
- Weather
- Timer
- Stopwatch
- Moon phase
- Shortcuts
- Widgets
- Gyroscope
- and more...
Additional animations in most of the watch faces;
Regular giveaways and upcoming designs showcase;
Excellent support.

Is it for me?
BOOST! Watch Face Collection is designed for Android Wear watches and works perfectly both for round and square models.
