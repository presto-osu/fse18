Description

Alien 3D Watch Face is a animated 3D watch face for Android Wear devices.
Works great on both rounded and squared watches. Customizable themes, colors, date format and more.


Features :

+ analog / digital mode for square and round watches
+ 6 themes background with 3D animation
+ parallax effect
+ random themes
+ random seconds sign
+ custom digits color
+ custom second sign color
+ custom clock hands color
+ 4 date format
+ time / date in analog mode
+ battery friendly
+ rounded and squared watches

Please rate. Thanks!

Please make sure your watch is running Android Wear 5.0


::: TIPS :::

1. If you can't see watch face in 'Android Wear'.

Please go to Android Wear settings (small white gear in top right screen) and click new option 'Resync apps'. This resync all available apps on your watch. And you will see 'lost' apps.
