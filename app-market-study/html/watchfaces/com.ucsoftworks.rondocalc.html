Description

Simple and minimalistic calculator for Android Wear with innovative round design.
Circle layout minimizes mistakes probability on input and works good with both round and square watches.
Calculator supports working with big numbers.

NOTE: the app is only for Android Wear. It hasn't functionality for phones/tablets.

This is the open source app. Source is available on GitHub:
https://github.com/GoodBadOkay/RondoCalc
