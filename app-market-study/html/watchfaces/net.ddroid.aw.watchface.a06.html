Description

Custom watch face for Android Wear

Features
- Awesome designer's watch face for you.
- Simple but useful design face! Designed by professional designer.

More watch faces
- Visit our exclusive watchface collections for Android Wear on the Play Store
http://goo.gl/58kqxS

For Android 6.0(and higher) phone users
Need to do following setup procedure after installation.
http://androidwearface.com/for_android6_users/

Installation
It will be installed on your watch automatically from your phone paired with your watch after a short time and please wait.

Settings:Support US style date format and UK style date format
- mm-dd (US style)
- dd-mm (UK style)

Settings:Support color mode changing
- Auto switch Color mode and Black and White mode (when dimmed)
- Always Black and White mode

Settings:Support 12Hours format.
You can choose between three types of time format.
- 12Hours ex. '08:35' with AM/PM
- 12Hours ex. '8:35' with AM/PM (No zero in front of times)
- 24Hours(military time)

Settings:Support color mode changing
- Auto switch Color mode and Black-and-White mode (when dimmed)
- Always Black and White mode

Settings:Support Black-and-White mode's color change
- You can select Black-and-White mode color from among seven colors.
Select 'Color filter at the time of the Black-and-White mode' on the setup screen.

Show wear's battery info
- Can show wear's battery level (0%-100%)

settings for IPS LCD Wear(Like G Watch)
Color mode drawing on dimmed mode


* Tap the watchface icon on your PHONE to setup.

Please note
- If you don't like this model please cancel your order in 15 minutes. And try another watch face.

This watchface is designed for Android Wear Rectangle Watch like following devices.
- ZenWatch (ASUS)
- Gear Live (SAMSUNG)
- G Watch (LG)
- SmartWatch3 (SONY)

Tested with LG G Watch,Samsung Gear Live

More watch faces
- Visit our exclusive watchface collection for Android Wear on the Play Store
http://goo.gl/RITByg

*Smartphone and Android Wear must be paired before you use it.
