Description

App launcher for Android Wear is a highly polished and personalized launcher for your Android Wear watch.

Features:

- Browse or launch apps from your Android Wear smartwatch
- Select one of many themes and customize it to look and act as you wish.
- Choose between: Quick launcher, iWear launcher, Grid launcher, Row launcher and more coming!
- Battery efficient!

Supports:

- LG G Watch
- Samsung Gear Live
- Moto 360
- LG G Watch R
- Sony SmartWatch 3
- Asus Zenwatch

Take your Android Wear watch to the next level.
