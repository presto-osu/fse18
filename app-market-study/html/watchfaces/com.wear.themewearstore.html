Description

Tempo-motion is a Fitness app that help you get fit using data and reward you when you do so.
-Using your Android wear devices or mobile phones,your fitness data will be visualized in a simple view within your touch,ready when you want ,where you want it to be.
- More importantly, We really reward you with gift cards credits toward your next purchase of a great movie at AMC, dinner at AppleBee , shopping experience at WholeFood and More to come.
- Make every steps counted toward a reward,a giftcard but more importantly a better YOU
#GetFitGetReward #MakeEveryStepCount
Compatible with : LG GWatch, Samsung Gear Live, Moto360, LG Gwatch R, Asus Zen Watch.
