Colorful watch face for Android Wear. New Color Every Hour.

Colors from Google Design Color palette.

Android Wear smartwatch only.

Optimized for round smartwatches such as the Motorola Moto 360.
Works at rectangular watches such as the Samsung Gear Live, the LG G Watch.
