Description

Animated Samaritan UI WatchFace for Android Wear & LiveWallpaper
base on the TV show Person Of Interest

For the LiveWallpaper, you can change:

- The background colour. (White and Black)
- The phrase display by Samaritan with one of the following
: WHAT ARE YOUR COMMANDS ?
: I WILL PROTECT YOU NOW
: CALCULATING RESPONSE
: INVESTIGATION ONGOING
: FIND THE MACHINE
- and more ....

For the WatchFace , you can change:
- The background colour. (White and Black)
- between blod and regular font
- 12/24 hours mode
