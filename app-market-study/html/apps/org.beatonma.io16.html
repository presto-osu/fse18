Description

Based on the stylish countdown timer for Google I/O 2016: https://events.google.com/io2016/

The app includes a highly customisable home-screen clock widget, live wallpaper, daydream, and watchface for Android Wear.


The app is not official or affiliated with Google in any way.


FAQ:
Q: Why isn't the widget fully animated?
A: Android widgets are not designed to animate and forcing them to do so seriously affects battery life. If you want to see the full animation please use the live wallpaper!


If you want to make suggestions for new features, post your favourite configuration, see the latest change log, or get help with any problems, please see my Google+ community at https://plus.google.com/u/0/communities/113955537219140914914/stream/ac2d6853-a264-4768-976d-e1f3181a6fa7



Permissions:
- In-app purchases: If you like the app you can leave a dollar (or equivalent in your currency) tip. All app content is free and tipping does not unlock anything new (except my gratitude <3).
- Bind to a dream service - Necessary for Daydream functionality.
- Wake lock - Necessary for Android Wear watchface functionality.
- Read external storage - Necessary on many devices for choosing a custom background image.



This project uses the fantastic uCrop image cropping library for handling background images. Check it out here: https://github.com/Yalantis/uCrop
