Description

The most complete, beautiful and simple app to follow stock market and financial markets.
Get quotes of stocks, funds, ETFs, currencies,futures and markets and manage your portfolios.

Completely free with no limitations, you can remove ads with optional in-app purchase.

-Realtime quotes and charts of worldwide stocks, funds, ETFs, currencies, futures and markets (funds, futures and currencies may be delayed).
-Material Design interface.
-Customizable interface colors can ease problems of color blindness.
-Android Wear app.
-Alerts when stock hits target price (in Android Marshmallow refresh could be delayed in doze mode).
-Detailed financial data.
-PreMarket and AfterHour quotes.
-News.
-Daily quote update frequency: save battery and data, refreshing data only when you want to. During the weekend data sync is automatically paused.
-All quotes are stored locally to save data traffic.
-Daily and historical charts.
-Technical analysis charts with indicators.
-Portfolio with gain\loss positions and charts.
-Multiple portfolios up to 6.
-Automatic conversion from portfolio currency to main currency using real-time exchange rates.
-Short selling positions supported.
-Widgets with light and dark theme (only for android 4 or superior).
-Backup\Restore data to SD.
-Backup\Restore on Google Drive.
-DashClock extensions.
-Keep your portfolio data safe with password protection.
-AutoApps for Tasker plugin (requires Tasker and AutoApps).
See https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bZPW0yRHRT4


About permissions
- Network access: obviously requested to update data.
- Wake-up phone: used only when an alert is set to notify the price target.
- Vibration: used only when an alert is set to notify the price target.
- Access to SD card: requested for backup-restore.

Tips
-Data are not updated\can't get notification in Android Marshmallow.
If a user leaves a device unplugged and stationary for a period of time, with the screen off, the device enters Doze mode. in Doze mode network access is disabled and tasks are suspended.
https://developer.android.com/preview/features/power-mgmt.html

-Data are not refreshed every 30 seconds in Android Marshmallow.
In Marshmallow minimum update frequency is 30 seconds.

-I can't find any stock in my market, why?
App uses free services to get quote data. Some markets like PSE are not covered and there's not much i can do. Those data are available only under subscription for professional purposes.
Please don't give a bad rate because of that, it's not my fault.

If you want to help me to localize it in your language goto https://www.localize.im/projects/lp

If you find this app useful..please rate it.
In case of crash please add your email to crash report so I can contact you.
