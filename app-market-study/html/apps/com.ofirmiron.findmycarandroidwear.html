Description

What is Find My Parked Car?
Find My Parked Car allows you to save your exact parking location and view it at a later time when needed. After parking your car, press the park button and your location will be saved. When you later need to see where you parked your car, you can open the app and view it's exact location.

Automatic Parking Detection
Find My Parked Car detects when you start walking after driving, and notifies you with your accurate parking location. The app also notifies you when you get near your car, and guides you to its location.

Features
Automatic Parking Detection
Take a picture of the parking location
Set an alarm for metered parking
Write a note with the floor number or street name
Android Wear support (both rectangular and round watches)
Turn-by-turn walking navigation
Parking History
Share parking location with others
Android N quick title
