Description

Ever wanted to troll your friends or be the center of attention at the next party? Do you want to be the ultimate master of the LULZ? Here is your chance!

With Remote Effects Pro you can remotely activate a variety of sounds directly from your Android Wear device and play them on your phone completely unnoticed! Underline your joke with the proper sound or play pranks on your friends and family.

With more than 150 different sounds, ranging from the fabulously funny to the awesomely annoying, youll have a sound for any occasion!

The remote soundboard is compatible with the LG G Watch R, the Samsung Gear Live, the Sony SmartWatch2, the Sony SmartBand and more.
