Description

Mobile application TaxovichkoF - taxi calling button in your mobile phone!

Taxi fare from 33 rubles
Car will arrive in 5 minutes (within the city)
Free Wi-Fi
Child safety seats
Smart phone chargers
Credit cards are welcome
Online payment + discounts


Silent taxi call
Is there a lecture or are you afraid of waking up a child or is it too noisy outside? Order a taxi through a mobile application without saying a word.

Urgent taxi order
Don't waste your time talking to the operator. Order a taxi to one of your Favourite addresses by tapping the screen only several times! Each option is at hand!

Off-line order
With the app it is possible to call a taxi even without Internet connection. Just fill in the form and all the data will be automatically sent by SMS.

Total control
The application allows to observe all TaxovichkoF opportunities and equip your own taxi: car type, additional options, addresses, pick-up time etc.


Main features:
Defining the users location
On-line car tracking on the map
Automatic fare calculation
Exact pick-up time
Trip time from A to B
SMS order without Internet connection
On-line payment without any change and tips + discounts


Additional opportunities:
Get information about the driver and influence on his rating
Add favourite routes and addresses
Popular addresses and nearest objects
Create pre-orders for the future
Use alarm-clock to wake you up before the trip
Order a taxi for another number from your contact list.
Message about forgotten things
