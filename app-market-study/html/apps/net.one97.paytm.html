Description

#PaytmKaro for the UPI, Savings Account, Mobile Recharge, DTH, Data & Metro Recharge, Movie Ticket, Train Ticket, Bus Ticket & Flight Booking. With Paytm app, it is simple to Send or Receive Money, use QR Code Scanner to scan all types of QR, create UPI to link your Bank account or open a savings account with Paytm Payments Bank

Paytm Payments Bank Savings Account
Open Savings account with Paytm Payments Bank and enjoy benefits like zero charges on online transactions, no minimum balance requirements and free virtual debit card. Keep upto Rs. 1 lakh of deposits and earn interest every month. Visit Paytmbank.com for more details.

Paytm BHIM UPI Address
Create your own Paytm BHIM UPI id on the app. Link any of your Savings account with this unique ID and start sending & receiving money instantly. You dont need bank details or IFSC code to send or receive money. Send upto Rs. 1 lakh in a single transaction using UPI and there is no receiving limit. There are zero charges for transferring money using UPI.

Online Recharge and Utility Bill Payment
Do mobile recharge or pay postpaid mobile bills for Aircel, Airtel, BSNL, IDEA, Matrix Postpaid, Tata Docomo, Tata Indicom, Vodafone, Telenor, Jio, MTNL and more. Pay electricity bills (CESC, Reliance, Tata Power, MSEDCL, BSES etc.), water bills (DJB, BWSSB, MCG etc.) or gas bills (IGL, SGL, Adani, Mahanagar, Siti, etc.) through the Paytm app quickly. Data card recharge and bill payment are available for Aircel, Airtel, BSNL, IDEA, Jio, MTNL, Vodafone, Tata Photon Plus and more.

Send or Receive Payment
Send money to friends & family or make a payment to any shop or merchant using a phone number or Paytm QR. You can pay using your wallet, UPI linked bank account or Paytm Payments Bank Savings account or you can add money to your wallet with debit cards, credit cards, net banking from all leading banks - using Visa, Mastercard, Rupay.

Offline Payments, QR Code Scanner
Pay through QR code scanner at restaurants, lifestyle & fashion outlets, grocery stores and so on. Use Paytm wallet or QR code scanner to pay for your cab/auto rides like Uber, Ola, Meru, etc. Explore places near you that accept payment through the Paytm wallet. Pay for your local Kirana shop, retail stores (like Big Bazaar, More, Central etc.) or even for restaurants, petrol pumps (Indian Oil Petrol pumps, Bharat Petroleum), milk (Mother Dairy) through Paytm.

Entertainment- Amusement Park, Event & Movie Ticket
Book movie tickets for the latest releases and upcoming movies across various theatres & multiplexes. You can also book tickets for events, amusement parks, water parks, theme parks and enjoy Cashback offers & discount deals.

Book Travel Tickets on Paytm Flights, Buses, Trains
Book your next flight ticket, train ticket or bus ticket with Paytm and enjoy Cashback offers! Complete your journey with a hotel booking on Paytm. Book IRCTC train tickets online and you can also check PNR status.

Metro Card Recharge
Make a metro card recharge (Delhi Metro, Hyderabad Metro, Mumbai Metro) with Paytm and skip long queues.

Digital Gold
Buy or sell digital gold with Paytm. To get physical gold against Paytm digital gold, you just need to pay the making charges and request for delivery anytime after 24 hours.

Loan Payment & Insurance Premium
Pay your loan EMI & take advantage of various promo codes available. Dont worry about payment delays, pay your insurance premiums in a jiffy with Paytm.

Shop Online
Shop for the top-selling products in women and men fashion, mobiles & electronics, home & kitchen, accessories, jewellery, home appliances, baby & kids and more.
