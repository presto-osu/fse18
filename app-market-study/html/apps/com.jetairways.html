Description

Welcome to the Jet Airways Mobile app for Android!

Now travel smart and stay connected with the all new Jet Airways mobile app. With this safe and secure tool you can seamlessly plan your trip, search for the lowest fares, check-in, view your flight status, discover special offers, manage your booking and your JetPrivilege account.

Highlights:

1. Flights Seamlessly book and pay for domestic and international flights using multiple payment options. With our My Booking feature, now save your itinerary details for easy access anytime
2. Manage Booking Amend your flight itinerary, change or cancel bookings, update your frequent flyer number, contact details, meal preference and even passport and immigration data
3. Redeem JPMiles Book an award ticket using JPMiles to your preferred destination
4. Seat Select - Select your preferred seat while booking your ticket or use our Manage Booking feature for the same
5. Check-in - Select your preferred seat and get your eBoarding Pass delivered to your email
6. JetPrivilege Enrol in our award winning frequent flyer program and access your JetPrivilege account on the go.
7. Flight Status - Check your flights status real time. Search using your flight number, airport or route
8. Flight Status Notification Subscribe and receive updates on the flight status for a particular flight
9. Feedback - Share your experience with us.
