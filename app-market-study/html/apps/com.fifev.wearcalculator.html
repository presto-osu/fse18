Description

Do you need more than a simple calculator? Then swipe to the left to get a page full of scientific functions on the Fife-V Wear calculator. Swipe again to bring the digits page back.

Youll have access to all these operators: %, 1/x, sin, cos, tan,sin-, cos- tan-, e, square root, pi, x!, x^2, x^3, x^n, +-

Wear Sci Calc is a calculator app for the Android Wear Platform from Fife-V.
