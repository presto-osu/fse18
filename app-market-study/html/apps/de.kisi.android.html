Description

The Kisi App turns your smartphone into an encrypted mobile key. Unlock your doors with the swipe of a finger or use Kisis NFC based Tap to Unlock technology. Learn how to get your own Kisi door devices via www.getkisi.com.

Functionality:
- Your company Badge in your phone
- See and manage mobile or card based access rights
- Tap to Unlock technology for faster, more secure access
- Remote unlock of door for administrators
- Set up and configure new Kisi gear
