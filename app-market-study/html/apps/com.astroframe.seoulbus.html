Description

Kakaobus provides you with real-time bus and bus stop information of 57 Korean cities.

* Real-time bus arrival information
* Bus route information with real-time bus location
* Departure Alarm
Set up the departure alarm to receive notifications five minutes, three minutes or one minute before the bus arrives at your stop.
* Arrival Alarm
Set an alarm to make sure you get off at the right stop.
* Bus Arrival Notification
You can get notifications for buses at a certain day and time without having to set them manually every time.
* Suggestions based on your current location : Nearby Stops, Night Buses Nearby, Direct Bus to Home, Kakao Taxi

Continued use of GPS running in the background can dramatically decrease battery life.

Edward Pontee July 15, 2016
This app used to be SeoulBus before being taken over by Kakao. The previous version used to be able to automatically scan the nearest bus stop with consistent reliability and list all incoming buses right away. It also had a MUCH more user-friendly interface. As a native English speaker with limited Korean, I was able to navigate the app with relative ease. The CURRENT Kakao update has now lost that reliability and ease of use. In terms of reliability, it scans the nearest bus stop less well, and it doesn't always list all possible bus routes--sometimes not at all. The interface has added annoying additional steps you have to input in order to find incoming bus info. You have to pinpoint your location on a GPS map, which may be confusing if there are multiple bus stops in your vicinity or you're not good with maps. You also have to input the direction you're traveling by inputting a bus stop along the route you want to travel, which is problematic if you don't know your bus route well. The worst is you need to connect your Kakao account to activate its "full" features. It seems like Kakao is more interested in data mining than maintaining a quality app....
