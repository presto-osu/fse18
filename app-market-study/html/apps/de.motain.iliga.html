Description

SOCCER
Never miss another match or the latest transfers news. Onefooball is THE football app to have. Are you a die-hard supporter of US soccer? Then follow the MLS, Lamar Hunt U.S. Open Cup and much more. Or are you also interested in international soccer? Then Onefootball has more than 200 major leagues and competitions to offer, such as the World Cup, Premier League, Liga MX, Primera Division and many more. Our favorite function allows you to customize Onefootball to your needs.

COMING SOON - RUSSIA WORLD CUP 2018

Onefootball is the only Soccer app you need for this summers World Cup in Russia 2018. Whether you support Brazil, Germany or Mexico, weve got plenty of content for our USA fans to tackle. Keep up with the latest news, scores, stats and key dates throughout the competition.

See the match schedule for the group stages and follow your team all the way to the knockout stages and beyond. Get real-time updates during games and read player profiles for each of the 32 qualified WC teams. Kick-off the worlds greatest tournament with us.

LIVE SCORES
Weve got you covered with live scores from the latest Soccer matches with all goals.

INSTANT PUSH NOTIFICATIONS
Be the first to get livescore updates or the latest transfer rumours with our instant push notification system.

FOOTBALL NEWS AND HIGHLIGHTS
Follow teams, players or competitions around the world and get personalised football news and videos.

COMPETITION, PLAYER, AND CLUB PAGES
From the Champions League to the MLS, Confed Cup to the FA Cup to 200+ more competitions, weve got you covered. Look up your favourite clubs and read up on all players.

ALL TRANSFERS
Be the first to know about transfer rumors and confirmed transfers including transfer fees.

FOOTBALL TABLES
With our standings, you can see instantly how many points your club has in the competition.

FIXTURES AND RESULTS
We show you upcoming matches and the results of the previous matches.

VIDEOS AND VIDEO CHANNELS
Sit back, relax and watch everything about your soccer club.

LIVE COMMENTARY
Follow our expert analysis on all major soccer matches including the MLS, the FA Cup, the Champions league and the World Cup qualifiers and you can vote for the best player of any of the matches.

PREDICT RESULTS
Predict results for upcoming soccer matches and share them with your friends. Who of you is the best football expert?
