Description

eCab, the smart city move.

Available in 10 cities in Europe: Paris, Brussels, Amsterdam, Lyon, Lille, Rennes, Bordeaux, Reims, Aix-en-Provence and Douai. More cities around the world will be soon available.

With eCab:
- Order in 1 click with your smartphone.
- Pre-book your vehicle up to 15 days in advance
- Estimate the price and duration of your trip
- A professional and thoughtful driver in 5 minutes
- Track your eCab in real time
- Pay from your smartphone
- Evaluate your driver

3 levels of service designed for your needs:
- eCab: immediate order
- eCab+: priority order, pre-booking, choice of the vehicle
- eCab VIP: exclusive service, VIP driver, luxury vehicles
