Description

This is a free 7 day trial version of the main MyAV app that can also control main other IP/IR controllable devices.
MyAV is an unofficial Denon & Marantz AV Receiver Wi-Fi remote control app. Denon and Marantz are trademarks of the D&M Holdings.
Includes the ability to rename, remote and reorder inputs, multi zone control and a DLNA browser.
This app can be viewed portrait or landscape (default) and is design with high res graphics for tablets.

Not only this but MyAV can also control a range of other products, (whilst still controlling the volume of your Denon or Marantz). Other devices include Blu-ray players, TV's, set-top boxes, cable & sat.

If you have IR on your Android device then this app can control Denon Blu-Ray & DVD Players too.

A full compatibility list can be found on our site:
http://myav.co.uk/compatiblity.htm
