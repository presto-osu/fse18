Description

Unofficial app
Get useful information on the Cape Town MyCiTi bus services such as current card balance and bus times. A crucial app for tourists, travellers and daily commuters.

Where's my bus?
get up-to-the-minute bus times for any stop (note: currently using schedule, not real-time info)
find the closest bus stops based on your location
Android Wear companion app provides this feature directly on your wearable

Timetables
see a detailed timetable of every route and stop

Fare calculator
calculate the cost of any journey
plan your journey with point-to-point directions

MyCiTi map
a map of all the routes, stations and stops
get directions to any stop or station

Scan your card*
get your current balance and other info directly from your myConnect card
see a detailed breakdown of all the trips you've taken

MyCiTi service updates
get notifications of real-time updates affecting MyCiTi

Widgets
always have an eye on your card balance by adding a widget to your home-screen

* requires your device has NFC
This app is unofficial and not endorsed by MyCiTi or the City of Cape Town.
