Description

Get Moven with Smart Banking and take control of your finances.

Whether youre buying groceries, dining out, or saving for something on your wishlist, Moven automatically analyzes your spending and gives you instant receipts and insights so you can spend, save & live smarter. Want a summary? Moven provides a Daily Digest recap of your spending yesterday.

How Does it Work?
The Moven Smart Banking card and app work together to help you track, understand and manage your spending and saving.
1 - Download the free Moven app
2 - Apply for Moven smart banking. Its FDIC Insured, and you can use the Moven debit card wherever MasterCard is accepted. Or, try Moven with your existing debit, checking, and credit card accounts.
3 - Add Money into your account. Direct deposit, checks and cash, Moven accepts them all.
4 - Pay with Moven. Swipe your Moven debit card or tap it with Samsung Pay or Android Pay on your phone. Receive spending insights instantly after every purchase to help you control your spending.
5 - Track with the App. The Moven app instantly categorizes each purchase, and shows you how much youve spent for the month.
6 - Control your spending. When youre aware of how much youre spending, its easier to keep your spending under control.
7 - Stash those gains into savings. See your personalized savings path, and afford your wish list faster.

Other Cool Features:
- Link your accounts. Use Moven to track your spending across all of your bank accounts and credit cards.
- Pay your friends. Send money to another Moven user instantly, for free, or pay a friend using email or text - even if they dont have a Moven account. Search your phone's Contacts for speed and reliability.
- Forget about fees. Access your money at over 42,000 ATMs with no surcharges. And Moven charges absolutely no monthly fees or overdraft charges!
- Use your Moven debit card with Samsung Pay or Android Pay to shop in stores or in apps.
- Widgets show your Moven balance, Spending Meter, and recent transactions at a glance.
- Android Wear users see their Instant Receipts and Spending Meter on the go.

Download Moven today!

Disclosures
The Moven debit card is issued by CBW Bank, member FDIC, so your money is safely held in a federally-insured account.
