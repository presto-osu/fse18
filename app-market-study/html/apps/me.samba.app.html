Description

Reactions brings social interaction to life by having reactions recorded as videos are viewed. Simply post a video and watch your friends/fans react. Get ready to be entertained!

Be on stage:
When you create and post content through the app, the experience is like seeing your audiences' reactions in real life.


Be in the spotlight:
See and be seen. Feel the excitement as you are about to watch a video - you are unaware of the content and are placed in the spotlight with the camera focusing on you. By having your reaction recorded to a video you are able to UNLOCK others reactions to the same video!


How to use?
Give and get reactions.


What you want to see?
Follow, invite and interact with your friends. Explore staff picks, celebrities, comedians, and much more.


Creating content:
Shoot a video or simply upload from your camera roll.


Keep it personal:
You can also send direct video messages to friends and capture their reactions to your content.


Like, LOL, OMG - WTF! :-)
After Reactions, when you look back the excitement you felt when counting LIKES, it will seem so bizarre...
