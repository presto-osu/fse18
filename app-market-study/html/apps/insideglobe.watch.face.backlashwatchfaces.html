What you get:
It monitors your heartbeat
It counts your steps.
It shows the temperature of your location.
Set the location settings in your phone.
Set the time interval to see the temperature or stop it temporarily.
It shows the battery status of your phone and watch

What is Unique?
It has a unique phone Unlock Counter. You could know the number of times you unlocked your phone in a day.
ON/ Off provided in the sensor settings let you save the battery
Attractive Designs and Color Faces
All operations are simple and you have to simply press and swipe to get the widgets
