Description

* Turns your smart watch into your fitness companion and helps you maximize the output of your workout without worrying about logging and entering each detail about every exercise.
* Wirelessly sync with the Actofit app on your smartphone to get detailed analytics on your workout and take your fitness to the next level.
* When you exercise, your motion is tracked and compared to the database of exercises you record, allowing Actofit to successfully detect your exercise and count the number of reps.
* Force, Power, Velocity is evaluated on every set and you can see the numbers on your mobile app along with your previous exercise logs to compare the progress made.
* Increases efficiency by giving you progression charts and shows you whats working and whats not. It not only tracks your exercises but it learns while you burn.
* Feel youve lifted more than the app detects? You can edit the amount of weights lifted to give you more tailored and accurate results.
* With the guided workout mode follow a workout program and achieve that dream body youve been meaning to obtain.
* Stay motivated by keeping tabs on the progress your friends make when you connect your app with Facebook.
