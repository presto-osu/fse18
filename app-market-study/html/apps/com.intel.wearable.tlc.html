Description

You are busy. You are constantly running, trying not to be late, trying to make the most out of your day.

midu Android app and watch face is your life changer - a top notch personal assistant that helps you run errands, complete your daily tasks, organize your to-do tasks, set reminders to yourself or to friends and family and stay ahead of your schedule.

Enjoy smart reminders that pop up not only at the right time but at the right place (e.g. When am I leaving home). Send reminders to anyone, asking them to buy something, be somewhere, or complete a task.

Easily navigate your day - midu is the ultimate calendar-like time management tool. Think of it as a brilliant task-manager, helping you organize tasks and to-do lists, in a post paper notes world.

Supercharge your day - when it comes to busy Android users, time management apps are a productivity key. midu is an all-in-one personal assistant that works with both time and location. midu to-do lists and task manager functionalities will help you get more out of your day, schedule tasks, and ensure you never forget a thing and always ahead of time!

midu uses GPS signal to allow you set reminders for when you are driving or when youve arrived at a specific place.

Forget old post-it note apps, midu makes the most out of your smart Android phone and wear.

Automate and optimize your tasks to boost your productivity. midu is, designed to increase efficiency.

One place for all your to-do tasks, personal notes, automatic reminders, and time-related alerts. Get leave now alerts, based on real-time traffic, via push notifications, and send reminders to friends and family via push notifications or SMS.

Set reminders to call someone, be somewhere, complete a task in a certain place, shop for something, and more.

Using an android wear watch? With the midu Android Wear watch face, you can also take quick actions on the go. Personalize your watch face experience with several different colors, and choose between an analog and digital display.

*Midu's Android Wear watch face is compatible with Asus ZenWatch, Fossil Q, Huawei Watch, LG G Watch R, LG G Watch, LG Watch Urbane, Michael Kors Access Collection, Moto 360, Samsung Gear Live, Sony Smartwatch, and TAG.

Daily, weekly, or monthly planner In todays world, calendar is just not enough. A calendar is only based on time, but your tasks are optimized based on location as well! midu task and to do manager considers both time and GPS location and by that allows you to make a lot more out of your day.
