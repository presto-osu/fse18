Description

CheckMyTrip brings all of your trip details together in one master view. Ideal for business travelers and frequent fliers to stay productive and enjoy unique local experiences. Essential for leisure holiday seekers to navigate unfamiliar destinations and maximize the fun. It's free, easy to use and loaded with trip alerts, planning tools and more to get you safely from door to door.

BEAUTIFUL FEATURES AWAIT YOU

SMART TRIP ITINERARIES - Stay Organized
Flight, hotel, car, rail, meeting, dining, tour & activity details organized into one elegant itinerary view
3 simple methods to add & organize your itinerary details: forward booking confirmation e-mails to trips@checkmytrip.com; enter booking reference with first and last name; or manually enter the trip information through a form

HANDY TRIP TOOLS - Be Prepared
Direct access to many airline check-in systems and seat maps
Real-time notifications via push, in-app and email of flight status, terminal and gate details plus layover times
5-day weather forecast for each point in your journey
Search and reserve taxi and parking options in 40 countries

AWESOME DESTINATION TRIP PLANNING SERVICES - Feel Like a Local
First-hand local discovery of places nearby through Foursquare
Search and reserve over 50,000 epic tour & activity options worldwide available through Viator
Search and reserve over 41,000 great restaurant options in major cities available through OpenTable

READY TO TRAVEL FEARLESSLY?
Getting started is easy. Create an account using your Facebook ID or e-mail address. Like Us on Facebook to access curated travel planning news, inspiration and tips.

We respect your privacy - [https://www.checkmytrip.com/cmtweb/web-desktop.html#/privacypolicy]

By downloading the app, you accept our Terms & Conditions of use - [https://www.checkmytrip.com/cmtweb/web-desktop.html#/termsandconditions]

WHO IS BEHIND CHECKMYTRIP?
CheckMyTrip is brought to you by Amadeus - the technology company powering the travel industry. We facilitate the entire travel journey, improving the travel experience so millions of people can travel fearlessly every year. For more information, visit amadeus.com

**IMPORTANT REMINDER**
CHECKMYTRIP IS NOT A TRAVEL BOOKING AGENCY

We only display information related to the trip details you add to your CheckMyTrip account. If you need to modify, cancel or source additional information about your air, hotel, car, rail, taxi, parking, dining or tour & activity booking - you must contact the provider directly for assistance.
