Description

The Dolce Gusto Timer application helps in the preparation of Dolce Gusto beverages in non-automatic machines indicating switching off the machine according to the chosen beverage using an audible alarm and vibrating the device.
Also includes records of beverages to control their personal stock of capsules, with the option to warn when the capsules are finished.
It has a control as being the preparation of beverages and monthly historical recent months.
Configuration screen to enable/disable alarm and vibration, volume verification.
Option of preparing the beverage using the level indicated in the capsule.

The trademark DOLCE GUSTO is a registered trademark of Socit des Produits Nestl S.A.. Dolce Gusto Timer is not an official application and its developer is not associated with any Nestl-affiliated company nor it is an authorized retailer of Nestl products or services.
