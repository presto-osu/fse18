Description

Manage your banking affairs any place, any time. Download the free Dutch ING Mobile Banking App and experience the comfort of secure and quick banking from your smartphone or tablet. Also available in English.

Advantages of the Mobile Banking App:
Get a quick overview of payments, savings , loans, investment and creditcard.
Easy money transfers , check transfer information and plan a savings deposit.
Choose how you want to log in or confirm transactions; with a chosen mobile PIN , your fingerprint or your voice.
Quickly check your balance , even on your smartwatch.
With Inge, you control the app easily by voice.
Stay informed with personal financial messages.
Take control of your daily limit for the app.
For private and / or business customers.

Secure mobile banking
With the ING Mobile Banking App you manage your banking affairs with a secure connection and no personal information is saved on your mobile phone or tablet. We advise you to always use the latest version of the app. It always has the latest features and meets current security standards.

Activating the Mobile Banking App
Using the app for the first time? It is a simple step by step activation. You need a Dutch ING payment account, a Dutch ING debit card and access to Mijn ING (internet banking). You can also register your business account in the app.

What do you think of the app?
Let us know. Tap on 'Feedback' in the app. With your experiences, we keep improving the ING Mobile Banking App
