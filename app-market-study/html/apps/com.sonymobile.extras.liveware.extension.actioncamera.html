Description

This is the official Sony Camera shutter application for your Sony SmartWatch 2, SmartBand SWR10 and SmartBand Talk SWR30.

With this app, you can control the shutter of your Action Cam and other cameras from Sony from a distance of up to 30 feet/10 meters. Take pictures using your SmartWatch, SmartBand or SmartBand Talk. You can also start and stop the video function in selected Sony camera models.

To use this app extension, you must have Smart Connect and/or the dedicated Sony app for your smart accessory installed on your Android smartphone or tablet.

When you have installed this app, it will not appear on your home screen. Instead, in case you are using SmartBand or SmartWatch 2 it will be integrated into Smart Connect. Smart Connect app from Sony required. In case of using SmartBand Talk, it will be integrated into the SmartBand Talk dedicated Sony app. SmartBand Talk dedicated Sony app required.

Works with: Sony Action Cam and a wide range of Sony digital cameras and DV cameras. See complete list of compatible devices at https://developer.sony.com/develop/cameras/device-support/.

If you want to develop apps or app extensions for innovative Sony accessories, go to www.sonymobile.com/developer.

Enjoy!
