Description

The KNWA Northwest Arkansas News app offers superior local News, Weather and Sports at home or on-the-go. You can count on KNWA to provide the latest news, weather, sports and more in Northwest Arkansas
Features:
- News, Weather, Sports, Photos, Videos and More
- View Community/Neighborhood News
- Geo-located Weather and Advertising
- Weather Central Interactive Radar
- Live Streaming Audio and Video
- Submit Photos and Videos
- Receive Alerts for Breaking News, Severe Weather, School Closings and More
- Share Content via E-mail, Text Message, and on Facebook and Twitter
