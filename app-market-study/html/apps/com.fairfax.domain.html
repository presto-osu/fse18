Domain Real Estate and Property Search

Find a property you'll love in Australia with Domain's real estate app for Android. Discover apartments and houses to buy, rent or sell Australia-wide while you're on the go.

Key Features:
- Find a Property: Search real estate for sale, rent, sell or new homes Australia-wide
- Amazing maps: Search and view properties via interactive maps
- Home Price Guide: Search 13 million properties in Australia and get a free property profile, including price estimate, sale history and a list of similar nearby properties that are active in the property market.
- Customise your search: Filter by price, bedrooms, bathrooms and other property features
- Property alerts: Save your favourite search and we'll alert you when new properties in your criteria become available
- Finger Search: Narrow down your property search, draw an area on your favourite part of Australia to find properties in the area
- Schools Zones: Now you can see which school catchment zones a property falls within!
- Locations & Nearby Places: Calculate travel times to your favourite locations & Pick nearby places that matter to you. Two great features to help you find your perfect home.
- Photo galleries: View property photos and floor plans on a full screen with a simple pinch gesture
- Inspection planner: Add properties to your inspection planner to view dates, times and maps
- Directions: Get directions to inspections and auctions
- Contact agents: Quickly get in touch with real estate agents about properties you're interested in
- Share listings with friends: Tell your friends via email, Facebook, Twitter and Google+
- Sync across devices: Log in to your Domain.com.au account to view your favourites, shortlist and saved searches on any device
- Track the value of your home to help you decide when's the right time to make your next move. Update the details about your home to improve the accuracy of your Home Price Guide estimate.
- Looking to Sell? Make the most of Domain tools for sellers.

Are you searching apartments or houses for rentals in Australia? Domain Real Estate app has the best set of tools to quickly find a property you'll love!

Looking for apartment and houses for Sale in Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth, Adelaide, Hobart, Canberra, Darwin or anywhere in Australia? Domain got you covered too, with thousands of listing of properties for Sale, Rental and Share.
