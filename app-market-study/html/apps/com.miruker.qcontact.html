Description

QuickContact is an address book and contact manager for use by professionals and individuals alike. The app functions equally as well on Android phones and tablets. Contacts are organized by groups and provide direct links to initiate calls or e-mails. The numerous settings allow a user to customize group and individual contact displays, as well as general functions, for maximum speed and efficiency.
