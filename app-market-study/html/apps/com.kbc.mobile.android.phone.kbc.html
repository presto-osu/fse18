Description

Don't fancy going to your branch to do your banking or don't have time? There's no need. With the KBC Mobile app, you can bank where and when you want. It's completely free of charge, really easy to use, 100% secure and packed with great features that let you pay online and withdraw cash with your smartphone, transfer funds in real time between your own accounts or to accounts at other banks, check your account balance and transactions, pay or get paid from one phone to another, and more besides.
You can even quickly open a savings account or Pricos pension savings plan, check your assets at a glance and get bespoke insurance quotes.
No wonder we've got over 800 000 very happy KBC Mobile users. Are you about to join them? Install it now and take it for a test drive in demo mode.
Enjoyed your test drive? Great! Just sign up using your debit card and card reader and you're off.
Learn more at www.kbc.be/mobile.
