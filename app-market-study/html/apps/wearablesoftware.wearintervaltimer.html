Description

An interval timer for training and workouts or work and rest times. This app is simple and easy to use. An awesome timer, whether you're working on cycling, running, lifting weights, exercise, workout, stretching or boxing. This app is simple and easy to use and a great helper for on the go. Download now for free. To start the app. Just say: OK Google...Start Interval Timer.


Get more apps, games & news about Android Wear with "Android Wear Center": http://tinyurl.com/m87stxs


Features:
Simple and intuitive interface
Set hours, minutes and seconds of the timer
Pause, resume, reset timer
Set rest time
Set repetitions
Set unlimited repetitions


You have to own an Android Wear device to use this app
Currently compatible devices:
HUAWEI Watch
Moto 360 Gen 2 (2015)
Moto 360 Gen 1 (2014)
Asus Zenwatch
LG G Watch
LG G Watch R
LG G Watch Urbane (Android Wear version)
Samsung Gear Live
Sony SmartWatch 3


Please note:
-Make sure your mobile is compatible with Android Wear.
-Make sure to pair your phone with the wearable device with help of the Android Wear App.
-To open: Go to "Start..." and open the app "Interval Timer" or say "Start Inverval Timer"
