Description

Use Desjardins mobile services to make your standard business or personal financial transactions securely and get financial information, no matter where you are. Certain functions also available on Android Wear.

Use AccsD mobile to:
deposit cheques by taking a photo of them, without having to go to your caisse or an ATM
make purchases with Android Pay.
check your caisse, credit card and prepaid card account balances without having to log on with InstaBalance, whether you are an individual or business member. Also available to non-members who are Desjardins credit or prepaid cardholders
turn on and receive Desjardins notifications
search transactions made in the last 12 months in your various caisse accounts by type and period
pay your bills, suppliers, credit card accounts and Accord D financing plan and repay your line of credit
add service providers and organizations for bill payments
transfer funds between your accounts and between individuals (including transfers to accounts of Desjardins credit cardholders) and add or delete recipients
make Interac e-Transfers
select a monthly plan or change your current one
open or close a High Interest S@vings Account or Enhanced Investment Business Account or invest in Term S@vings
renew your market-linked guaranteed investments and Term Savings deposits
create, track, modify a project as part of My Savings Plan or support a loved ones savings plan
use the Hop n S@ve instant savings tool and receive notifications to help you reach your goals
get access to Desjardins credit card cash advances
apply for a credit card or mortgage loan
use the My budget tool to manage your budget
make an RRSP contribution to your Regular Savings Account RRSP
see, modify or delete pending transactions
register for AccsD
reactivate your AccsD password
contact a Desjardins advisor by secure message, whether with your caisse or other Desjardins Group components

You can also use Desjardins mobile services to:
access new content like financial tips and information about various products and services
find a caisse, Desjardins Business centre or ATM
get the phone numbers to reach us
use the currency converter
check rates
follow the stock market and trade securities on the Disnat mobile site
check Desjardins job opportunities
access the Desjardins assistance services and Ajusto mobile apps
request a car or tenant insurance quote
see Desjardins Member Advantages
open an account and become a member

N.B.: Features available on AccsD mobile and AccsD Affaires mobile may vary.
Interac e-Transfer is a registered trademark of Interac Inc. Used under license.
