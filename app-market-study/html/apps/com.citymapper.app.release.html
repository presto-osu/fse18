Description

EDITORS CHOICE

Citymapper is the ultimate transit app, making cities easier to use!
+ Check nearby departures in real-time
+ Find the fastest route combining bus, subway, train, ferry, taxi, car share, bike share and walk
+ Follow step by step directions, including the best subway exit to reach the platform
+ Receive alerts when your favourite lines are disrupted

"Citymapper is, quite simply, the best travel app" - New York Times
Nominated for Best App 2017 - Google Play Awards
#1 Grand Prize Winner - MTA New York App Quest
Grand Prix Award - TechCrunch

THE SMART TRIP PLANNER
FIND THE BEST ROUTE - Our trip planner combines all transport modes in your city to find the fastest route: bus, subway, train, ferry, cab (Uber, Lyft, taxi), car share, bike share and walk.

REAL TIME DEPARTURES - Live bus and train times for MTA, NJ Transit, LIRR, Metro North, SF BART, MUNI, Caltrain, Chicago CTA, Boston MBTA, and more.

ALERTS - Be notified of delays and disruptions for your favorite lines of the NYC subway, LRR, Washington Metro, Chicago L, MBTA subway, BART, Metra.

STEP-BY-STEP DIRECTIONS - Follow your trip in real-time with step-by-step directions, including live bus times and an alarm when it's time to get off the bus.

OFFLINE MAPS - Official NYC subway map, Manhattan bus map, Brooklyn bus map, Queens bus map, DC metro map, Muni Metro map and more.

MULTIMODAL - Combine cab with transit to create new routes you never knew existed.

+ Real-time bike share availability for Citi Bike, Bay Area Bike Share, Divvy, Hubway, CaBi, Breeze, Metro Bike Share, Indego
+ Real-time car share availability with Car2go in NYC, DC and Seattle
+ Get directed to the best pick up spots for your cab Uber or Lyft
+ Bus tracker for MTA, MBTA and more.
+ Includes NYC transit fares and SF transit fares
