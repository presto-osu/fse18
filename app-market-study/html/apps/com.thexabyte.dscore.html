Description

Dscore is a sports application that gives you live scores on your watch face, allowing you to follow your favorite games at a glance from your Android Wear device. There are 9 unique watch faces to choose from and we are constantly adding new sports and wearable watch faces. Download and make your watch face a live scoreboard today!

FEATURE
- Live Scores
- Useful Vibrations: Period changes, goals
- User Friendly
- Minimal Data transfer
- Multiple Watch Faces
