Description

Podcasts To Go is nice looking pocket casts downloader, podcast manager and podcast player (aka podcatcher). It has neat and clean UI designed to make daily podcast listening as easy and smooth as possible.

--- We want to hear feedback from you! Help us make this app even better! ---

Major app features:

Easy to use podcast player user interface
- Moderate design focused on pocket cast daily listener tasks
- no crazy colors, no useless user experience bells and whistles
- fully following Android 4.x UI design guidelines
- new episodes notification - you are always notified about new podcast episodes

Playback
- playback controls available in notification area and lock screen too
- in notification and lock screen you can see podcast name and podcast icon
- podcast player can be controlled from lock screen

Auto download of subscribed podcast channels
- periodic download of new podcast episodes (can be turned off in options)

Podcast Discovery
- app allows to add podcasts either by iTunes directory search or by podcast rss feed url

Android Wear Support
- app can be remotely controlled from your Android Wear compatible Smart Watch
- please report incompatibilities and suggestions for improvement

We hope that this app will be best podcast player you ever met. Feel free to contact us with any suggestion how to make app better.
