Description

Get the most out of your runs, walks, rides, and more with our top rated fitness tracker and personal training app. With Endomondo, you can track all of your workouts using GPS, check your stats, and reach your fitness goals. Download the app now and get motivated to get active!

Heres what you get free of charge:

TRACK YOUR FITNESS
- Track your running, walking, cycling, and 40+ other sports using GPS
- See duration, speed, distance, calories, and more on your workout summary
- Get audio feedback on distance and pace for every mile/km
- Log your indoor workouts manually
- Track your heart rate (works with Bluetooth heart rate monitors)

ANALYZE YOUR PERFORMANCE
- Keep a full training log
- See how much you train each day
- Analyze performance such as split times for each workout

SYNC WITH APPS AND WEARABLES
- Get your calorie and nutrition information from MyFitnessPal right in the app
- Connect with Google Fit to add workout data to your dashboard
- Use your favorite wearable devices to track, including Garmin, Gear, Pebble, and Android Wear

SET GOALS AND TAKE ON CHALLENGES
- Set a time, distance or calorie goal, and let our audio coach guide you towards it
- Commit to fitness goals and let your friends motivate you
- Up the stakes on your workouts and win prizes with challenges

GET SOCIAL
- Send and receive real-time audio pep talks from friends
- Check the news feed to comment on friends workouts
- Race against a friends personal best time
- Share results and workouts on your favorite social networks: Facebook, Twitter, and Google+

AND MORE
- Add pictures, tag your friends, enable automatic pause function, customize your home screen, use countdown, and much more

All your data is automatically sent to your profile on www.endomondo.com. Here, you can analyze your data, dive into your stats, track your progress, as well as interact and compete with a global community of fitness enthusiasts.

So, whether youre into running, cycling, walking, kayaking, mountain biking, or any one of our 40+ sports...get up, get active, and free your endorphins!

Want to take your fitness even further?

GO PREMIUM

With Endomondo Premium, youll gain access to exclusive training features from just 2.50 USD a month.

Get Premium and:
- Reach your running goal with a personal training plan - whether its a 5K, 10K, or marathon
- View statistics, including calories burned per month, running distance by year, etc.
- Analyze heart rate zones for every workout (requires a compatible heart rate monitor)
- View weather information on each workout summary
- Enjoy an ad-free experience
- Get access to 10+ additional motivating features

The fine print:
Should you choose to upgrade to Endomondo Premium, payment will be charged to your Google Play account upon confirmation of purchase. The monthly subscription costs USD 5.99 while the annual subscription costs USD 29.99 per year or USD 2.50 per month.

Your subscription renews automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Auto-renewal may be turned off in your Account Settings in Google Play after purchase. For our full terms and conditions and our privacy policy, please visit: http://www.endomondo.com/terms and http://www.endomondo.com/privacy

Endomondo is powered by Under Armour Connected Fitness the worlds largest health and fitness community. The complete suite of Under Armour apps, built to empower healthy and active lifestyles, includes Endomondo, UA Record, MapMyFitness and MyFitnessPal.

NOTE:
We ask for the permission ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION to enable GPS tracking of your workouts.
We ask for permission to access your personal information and read contacts. This enables you to connect with your friends, challenge them, and follow them directly on your phone. We will read names and email addresses from your contacts ONLY if you actively choose to do so within the app.
