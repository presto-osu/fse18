Description

Passman is a mobile phone password manager. Store your passwords and keep them organized. Now with the incredible password explosion feature!
Free version, limited to 6 records.

Beside the 120 icons already included in the application, you can now use images and photos on the phone!

Passman iscompatible with your Android SmartWatch. It automatically syncs all your passwords and can be used even if the watch is not connected to the mobile phone!

Store unlimited passwords in the SQL Lite Database.
AES 128-bit Encryption.
Encrypted Backup and restore files.
Inactivity timeout.
Self-Destruction (the original!).
Password generator and strength checker.
