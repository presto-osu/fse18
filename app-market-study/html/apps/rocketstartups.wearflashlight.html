Description

The first flashlight app for the Android Wear operation system. Use your wearable as flashlight. It is simple and easy to use, a great helper for on the go. Download now for free. To start the app. Just say: OK Google...Start flashlight.


Get more top apps, games & news with "Android Wear Center": http://tinyurl.com/m87stxs


You have to own an Android Wear device to use this app
Currently compatible devices:
HUAWEI Watch
Moto 360 Gen 2 (2015)
Moto 360 Gen 1 (2014)
Asus Zenwatch
LG G Watch
LG G Watch R
LG G Watch Urbane (Android Wear version)
Samsung Gear Live
Sony SmartWatch 3


Please note:
-Make sure your mobile is compatible with Android Wear.
-Make sure to pair your phone with the wearable device with help of the Android Wear App.
-To open: Go to "Start..." and open the app "Flashlight" or say "Start flashlight"
