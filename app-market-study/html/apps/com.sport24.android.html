Description

Find out all the real-time sports wherever you are with Sport.24: results, matches, calendar of events and live rankings. 24/7: 25 sports and 75 events. Soccer, rugby, tennis, formula 1, handball, basket, motorcycle, cycling

Sport24.com application is arranged visually to afford quick and comfortable reading: Fast, modern, fluid and fun!
Lateral navigation : Easy and fast slide between sport home
Configure your application and access to your favorite news by mouse click

Stay updated about more than 100 sports and covered live events: soccer (Champions League, Liga, Premier League, Bundesliga, Football World Cup, French Football Cup), tennis (ATP, Rolland Garros, Wimbledon, Davis Cup), basket (NBA, Pro A, Euro League), rugby (Top 14, 6 Nations, Rugby World Cup), golf, handball, biking (Tour de France, Giro dItalia), formula 1, volleyball, motorcycle, rally

All the real-time sports news at your finger tips:
Access to the last news: all the live sport news about your athletes, your teams, the Mercato and the results of your favorites competitions.
Be constantly informed thanks to the Flash
Matches Live scoring and live commentary. See the latest scrores and rankings of your favorites teams. Never miss a beat!
Live the sport through the section Multimedia. You can watch daily updated videos and pictures and highlights from recent games of the leading European leagues, or from other types of sports such as tennis, rugby, basketball, formula 1
All the news about your favorite soccers teams (PSG, OM, Real Madrid, Manchester United, Milano AC, FC Bayern Munchen), rugby (Saracens, Leicester, Harlequins, Northampton, Gloucester) and all the news of your favorite players.

Find all the Sport24.com various headings :
NEWS : last news about your favorite team, your favorite player and your favorite competition
FLASH : to keep up to date with all the latest real-time news
MULTIMEDIA : new picture and new video everyday
LIVE : matches live scoring
CALENDAR : competition Schedule
STANDING : live standing of your favorite competition
