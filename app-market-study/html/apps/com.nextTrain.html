Description

Next Train Ireland is a simple, slick app that will quickly show you train times for any station in Ireland. Incorporating a modern UI that follows the Material Design guidelines, the app has lots of great feature such as:

- Comprehensive journey planner showing detailed itinerary information for any journey between two stations
- Fast realtime station lookup with detailed train information to show you what stations a train will call at
- Android Wear support
- Travel news from Irish Rail on Twitter

Please note this app is unofficial and not affiliated with or endorsed by Irish Rail.
