Description

Are you annoyed about taking your phone out of your pocket every time you want to cross something off your shopping list app?
We have the solution! With our new Tickit app you can manage your shopping list directly from your smartwatch (Samsung Gear Live, LG G Watch, Moto 360). Everything is right at your wrist.

Time is not standing still. In the upcoming weeks we are going to provide you with additional updates.

- Support for additional languages
