Description

Delivery helps you keep track of your packages on your Android Wear watch. Delivery currently supports: UPS, FedEx, USPS, and DHL. If you know any free tracking API, please let me know. Thanks!


CREDIT: Thank Boxoh for providing the new package tracking API: http://www.boxoh.com/api/docs/
