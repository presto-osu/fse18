Description

What's not to like about a Nixie tube powered watch?

Features low-power Ambient mode and a neat flicker effect for added realism. Compatible with both round as well as rectangular watches.

Warning: Currently doesn't support 2nd gen smartwatches such as Huawei, Moto2, etc. I'm working on fixing this.
