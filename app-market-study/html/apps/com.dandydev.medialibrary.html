Description

Easily catalog your record and/or CD collection and browse it on your Android device. Add titles by barcode scan or via the integrated Amazon.com search. Sort and search to quickly locate titles on the shelf. Use the coverflow view to effortlessly flip through your collection. Add custom album art by taking a picture or selecting from the gallery. Use the wish list to keep track of albums you want to purchase. Quickly load albums from a Google Docs spreadsheet or export your collection to share with others. Full tablet support. View your wish list on your Android Wear device.

Version History:

3.2
Added support for Marshmallow (Android v6.0) runtime permissions. Sprinkle on a bit more Material Design. Various bug fixes.

3.1.4
Added ability to specify ascending or descending sort order. Various bug fixes.

3.1.3
Alphabetical sort is now case-insensitive and ignores "the" in band names. Fix bug with media type and shelf selections when editing titles. Fix crash when selecting a search result on Android 5.0 Lollipop.

3.1.2
System and navigation bar colors change based on album cover on KitKat and Lollipop. Album covers animate between list and detail view on Lollipop. Various bug fixes.

3.1
Add release date and label to album detail & edit screens. Add menu option to add album manually for titles that do not return a matching search result. Load "Tracks & Other Info" link in app instead of launching external web browser.

3.0
Updated to Material Design (even more to come)! Moved shelf list into left navigation drawer. Cover art images used as source for action bar and font colors, giving each album a fresh look. Added ability to sort by release date (note that only newly added titles will have the date available for sorting). Various fixes to retain scroll position/selection on orientation change.

2.7.3
Fix rotation bug on title list screen.

2.7.2
Fixed issue that was preventing album art from being changed on some devices.

2.7.0
Moved less frequently used menu items into Settings screen. Improved flow for adding albums to your collection.

2.6.0
Jetzt im Deutsch! (Now in German!)

2.5.2
Added Android Wear support. The first ~12 items in your wish list will be displayed on your smart watch when the wish list is opened on your phone.

2.5.1
Fixed image sizes on high resolution devices such as the Nexus 5. Improved stability of Google Drive integration. Better reporting of crashes to more easily identify trends and prioritize fixes.

2.5
Fixed image sizes on high resolution devices such as the Nexus 5. Improved stability of Google Drive integration. Better reporting of crashes to more easily identify trends and prioritize fixes.

2.4.3
Updated to latest version of Google Drive SDK to fix breaking changes that were causing spreadsheet exports to fail.

2.4.2
Bug fixes for importing large collections and issues that can occur on initial launch on some devices.

2.4.1
Fixed issues with Google Drive upload when creating import/export spreadsheets. Revised import process to load from a comma separated value (CSV) file on the SD card as a workaround for lack of support for downloading a Google Drive spreadsheet as a CSV (see updated instructions on import tab). Added "title already exists" confirmation when scanning barcodes.

2.4
Ability to add more than one copy of an album. A confirmation prompt is now displayed instead of the "title already exists" message.

2.3.1
Resolved an issue with incorrect buttons being displayed on the action bar when editing a title on some devices.

2.3
Added menu option for submitting suggestions/ideas. Moved "Search" button to action bar for a more intuitive flow when adding titles (changes to "Save" after a search result is selected). Fixed issues with spreadsheet import/export from/to Google Docs.

2.2.1
Improved process for upgrading from free version. Various bug fixes.
