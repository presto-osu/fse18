Description

Arrive on time with Arrivo Sydney Lite!


Now supporting Android Wear. Favourite stops and their next departures show up on your Android Wear watch if you are nearby. Manage your commute from your wrist.

Arrivo Sydney is your powerful commuting companion providing Realtime departure and location information for Sydney Buses, Trains, Ferries and Light Rail.

Realtime departure information takes your commute to the next level, helping you make your travel time and decisions more efficient. Should I take the Bus that is approaching or do I wait for the Express Bus that is just around the corner?

Imagine no more waiting at the Bus Stop. Take the guess work out of wondering when your Bus will arrive. Just download Arrivo Sydney and check on your Bus in Realtime!

Even when you are offline without a data connection you still have access to the scheduled departure information stored locally on your device.

With Arrivo Sydney in your pocket you have access to Sydney's Bus, Train, Ferry and Light Rail information anywhere and anytime.

Arrivo Sydney makes your commute easy with a variety of benefits:
* Favourite Stop Proximity Alerts: Get a notification when you are nearby one of your favourite stops.
* Realtime Location information so you know where your Bus or Train is right now.
* Realtime Departure information so you know when your Bus or Train will get to your Stop/Station.
* Follow your Bus, Train, Ferry or Light Rail on the map and check on its progress.
* Save your Favourite Routes & Stops for convenient access and referral.
* Accessibility information for train stations
* Immediately find the next departure for your Stop - by Route, by Stop name or from your saved Favourites.
* Use predictive search from the action bar on the top of the app to quickly find your Route or Stop.
* View next Departures for Sydney Bus Stops, Light Rail Stops, Train Stations and Ferry Piers.
* View your Trip details so you know where & when to get off.
* View the Stops for your Trip on the map.
* Arrivo Sydney works anytime and anywhere. If you do not have a data network connection to get the Realtime updates you can still access the scheduled departures.
* The scheduled timetable information is stored on your phone.


Get access to Realtime departure and location information and download Arrivo Sydney Lite today!


Please note that this is the lite version with features limited for demonstration purposes. It is also ad supported. Please check out our full version (https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=co.arrivo.syd) to experience the app with the full feature set and without ads.
