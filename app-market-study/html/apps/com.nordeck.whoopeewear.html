Description

Just select the fart on your Android Wear watch or phone and start pranking people with the worlds most expensive whoopee cushion. Add or record your own farts from your device to give a personalized experience by clicking the plus button in the top bar.

Two modes of control:
1) Turn on the "Whoopee Notification" on the phone. Then view the notification on your watch and click "Open on Phone".
2) Use the "Whoopee Wear" application directly on the watch by launching it.

Whoopee Wear uses the system media volume, so before farting make sure the volume is all the way up.

Additionally Whoopee Wear can be used to locate a missing phone.
