Description

Control your power bill from your pocket.

With Powershop you know exactly how much power you're using and what it costs all before you have to pay for it. This means you can take action anywhere you like to save money and power.

Save energy and fight the rising cost of electricity by downloading the Powershop App and joining Powershop now.

If you're already a Powershop customer download our new App to...
* see how much power you're using and what you need to buy
* make sure you never miss a special
* forecast future usage and cost
* shop for Powerpacks to manage your budget
* compare and gain insights into your usage


If youd like to report any problems email a screenshot or the contents of the error message to info@powershop.com.au. Alternatively give us a call on 1800 462 668.


== Explanation of permissions required for the Powershop App ==
* Storage - modify or delete the contents of your USB storage: Cache certain images to load them faster for you.
* Camera - take pictures and videos: Required for you to turn the flashlight on and off while inputting meter readings on devices with a camera flash.
* Your social information - read your contacts: You can use your contacts to refer to Powershop through the Friend get friend promotion. The App will only ever access your contacts if you choose to use them for the Friend get friend promotion.
* Your accounts - find accounts on the device AND Affects Battery - Control vibration, prevent phone from sleeping):
Required to send you Push notifications for things like new powerpack special is available, meter reading reminders, Autopayment and Autopurchase reminder, etc.
