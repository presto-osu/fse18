Description

Simple but effective this application allows you to view schedules of Paris public transportation.
- RER
- Transilien
- Bus
- Paris Metro.
- Tram
- Noctilien
- Location of bus and metro stops

Also contains the network plans and a traffic bulletin

Note: To access the geolocation, a minimum OpenGL version is required, as well as Google Play Services.

If you encounter any problems or if you have any suggestions please do not hesitate to contact me by email!
