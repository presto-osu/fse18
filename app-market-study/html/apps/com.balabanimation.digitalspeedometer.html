Description

**GPS Must be enabled**

Direction now works more reliably on various devices as well as the mph/kph setting being saved upon exit.

Displays GPS Satellites that have a fix and are working on getting your speed, direction traveling, accuracy of GPS fix, and of course speed.

Certain devices have GPS issues that are hardware related, please take this into account when using on this device. It may take longer to get a good signal, and may be spotty when it does.

This app should only be used if properly secured to be hands free and not in a position to cause distraction. Please drive carefully and only use this to enhance not inhibit your ability to drive.

Displays your current speed in either Miles per Hour or Kilometers per Hour. Displays your top speed achieved. Use to accurately find out how fast you are going using your device's GPS.

Be aware, this is only as accurate as your phone's hardware and GPS must be enabled. Recent changes to the Android OS may cause a longer than expected time before a signal is detected.

Ability to customize background and text color and the size of the speed to optimize viewing on your Android device.
