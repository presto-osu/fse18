Description

The Kitsilano School App is an Android phone application developed by the Kitsilano App Club. As a student hobby club, the Kits App Club has been working on this application since October 2013. We will continue to update, improve, and expand our App to better serve the student body at Kitsilano Secondary School. We welcome any ideas and suggestions.

Current functionality of this application includes
- Countdown to the next bell
- Day1/Day2 calendar
- Automatic mute device during school hours option
- Class schedule
- Homework reminder
- Android Wear notification
- School announcement
