Description

SkinSwitch is an easy way to manage your Minecraft skins right from your smartphone or tablet.

It allows you to quickly add your favourite Minecraft skins from an online gallery, from a player's existing skin, or directly from anywhere else on the web. You can then log into your Minecraft/Mojang account and upload them in a couple seconds!

The app relies on an easy-to-use Material interface and is compatible with any device that runs Android 4.0+.

SkinSwitch also comes with an Android Wear app that allows you to change your skins even more easily just by using your voice. Just say "Ok Google, start SkinSwitch", and say the name of the skin you want to wear!

The app only works with the PC version of Minecraft, and is not compatible with the Xbox 360 version or Pocket Edition of the game! You must also have bought the full game, we cannot support cracked versions of Minecraft.

SkinSwitch is not affiliated with Mojang in any way, but we still love them. <3
