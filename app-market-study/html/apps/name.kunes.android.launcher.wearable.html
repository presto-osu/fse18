Description

The ultimate simplified interface - now for your smartwatch.

Compatible with all full-featured Android smartwatches, including IconBIT Callisto 100, Omate TrueSmart, ZGPAX S8.
NOT compatible with Android Wear watches like Samsung Gear Live, Moto 360, Sony Smartwatch and G Watch.

Customize your Android smartwatch to suit your needs. BIG Launcher Wearable uses large buttons and texts for all the functions of your smart watch - it isnt just a launcher. All the features are enlarged, easy to read and easy to use.

Phone call log
Dialer
Contacts with contacts editor
SMS messaging
Full screen SMS notifications
Apps list with quick search
Variable screen layouts
Customizable backgrounds
Downloadable color themes
Customizable buttons
Third-party icon packs support
Widgets & shortcuts support
Speak on long press (requires Talkback)
Password protected settings

Everything is included in one app. Everything is fast and simple. 40 languages supported.

This version features two screen layouts optimized for wearable devices - 2x2 and 1x1 buttons.

All other features are the same as in the BIG Launcher Senior Phone - an award winning app with more than 30.000 satisfied users.

Be sure to try the BIG Launcher Senior Phone FREE DEMO before buying or visit http://biglauncher.com for more information and videos.
