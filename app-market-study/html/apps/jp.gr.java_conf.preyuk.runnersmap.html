Description

Runners map can display map-based information in running, walking, cycling, etc.

In particular, has been optimized to Below.
android wear (smart watch) like motorola moto360 and SONY smart watch3.
wristwatch-type android terminal like SmartGear49.

- Overview -
When walking or running or cycling, this app can display routes ,Lap point and the current position on the map.

- Other features -
Compatible with voice notificattion. (English and Japanese)
Compatible with Android Wear ( smart watch ).
Compatible with Bluetooth Heart rate monitor. (Android 4.3 or later)
Compatible with Cycling Speed & Cadence sensor. (Android 4.3 or later)
Can customize free the content of Display item.
In Lap browse mode after activity, can display Lap in variety ways.

Can display the remaining time until sunset (or sunrise) in real time.
Can check current weather, tempreture, humidity and etc.
Can import from past records and PC.
Can export to Google Earth and Garmin Connect. (gpx support)
