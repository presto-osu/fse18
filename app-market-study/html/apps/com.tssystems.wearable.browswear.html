Description

This app allows you to browse the internet and view websites on your watch. The app removes most of the layout and keeps the text content in focus.

Features:
- Open an url (via voice-command)
- Open recent webpages
- Send a page from your phone (use the Share command in your web browser).
- Bookmark pages (premium)

How to bookmark (premium):
1. Open the webpage on your watch
2. Swipe to the right, and choose "Add Bookmark"

This app is currently in beta. If you have suggestions or issues, feel free to share them!
