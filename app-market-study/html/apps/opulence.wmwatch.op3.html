Description

Opulence OP3 Chronograph Watch Face for Watchmaker on Android Wear.

YOU NEED TO BUY WATCHMAKER PREMIUM TO USE ON YOUR WATCH. http://goo.gl/FMxUfY

Compatible to all Android wear Smartwatch and adapt to the Moto 360 Flat tire.

Features
2 selectable alliage gold and silver
14 Selectable colors + White and Black
12 Hour analog chronograph
3 Main dial digits mode 0h, 12h and 24h
In 24h mode the dial change from 12h to 24h after 13h
Phone and watch power levels
Animated mechanical day wheel
Animated mechanical date gear ring
Hands with dynamic highlights and shadows
Dimmed mode color follow the selected one
On the Moto 360 the 6 is removed for a better fit

Usage
Alliage change on Opulence logo
Color change on the 12h mini dial
Activate Chronograph on the date window
Dial time mode on the 30 minutes mini dial
Chronograph Star/Stop on the 12h mini dial
Chronograph Reset on the 30 minutes mini dial

Chronograph functions
Seconds hand ticks 5 times a second
30 minutes dial ticks every minutes
12 hour dial ticks every 30 minutes
When running the 2 small hands raise as an indicator
