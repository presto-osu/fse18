Description

iWear is ICICI Banks Android Wear Banking application through which you can stay connected with your ICICI Bank account via your Smart Watch.
iWear is a companion app that works only when user has downloaded Android Wear to their mobile devices.
iWear offers banking and informational services to make a simplified banking experience as never before.

On your Smart watch, you can view the following details:
Information about your Bank and Credit Card accounts
Balance details and last 3 transactions
Recharge your prepaid mobile number

Steps for Installation:

Step1 Download iWear app on your Android Phone.
Srep 2 Enter your Mobile Number on the first screen of the app and Set a four digit MPIN. You will receive a 6 digit activation code.
Step3 Please enter 6 digit activation code on the next screen.
Step4 You are ready to use the app now. Use the four digit MPIN to use the app
Step5 Select the app on the Watch and use the app.

Download iMobile, ICICI Bank's flagship transactional app from www.mobile.icicibank.com/dl
