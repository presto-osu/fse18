Description

1. Mic. Zoom is the brand new technology which automatically capture sound from distance.
2. Now Capture Sound like broadcast quality.
3. Turn Mic. Zoom On/Off and control the level dynamically
4. All Recorded mp3 files are now password protected.
5. Set Duration, Frame Rate, Bitrate and Encoding Quality according to your needs.
6. Play the recorded files in watch (is speaker available)
7. Send the recorded files to your paired mobile phone.
