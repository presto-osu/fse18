Description

Bluenote is a minimalist yet powerful notepad app for your Android device.With Bluenote you can quickly write notes,to-do lists and write down all your ideas and reminders.

Get organized
Organize your notes by tags and color to keep track of all your to-do lists, lecture and meeting notes, vacation plans, or anything you want to organize or remember.

Always up-to-date
Synchronize your notes and to-do lists on multiple devices with Dropbox

Reminders
Add reminders to notes and lists.

Android Wear Support


* Pro Features *
Ad Free
Night Mode
Calendar view.

* Features *
Note widget.
Markdown Preview
Export notes as text to SD
Import / Export notes to SD
Search notes
Sort notes and lists



* Icon is by Jovie Brett Bardoles.Check out his work at http://dribbble.com/bretbardolees
