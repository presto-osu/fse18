Description

Looking for the best deal on concert tickets for your favorite band? Trying to find cheap sports tickets to MLB and NFL games this season? Rukkus has you covered, searching tickets from hundreds of vendors to help you grab great seats for the cheapest ticket prices available. Browse upcoming events and buy tickets for concerts, sporting events (MLB, NFL, NHL, NBA), and broadway shows and reserve your tickets in two taps.

FEATURES:
Want to checkout in just two clicks? Rukkus supports Android Pay at checkout! Get to your events faster than ever.
Search over 1,000 ticket vendors at once.
Beautiful interactive seating charts with first-person seat views.
Recommended events based on your music tastes and favorite artists.
Checkout in just 2 taps. Save even more time by using our card scanner.
Mobile ticket entry for venues accepting e-tickets. No printed tickets required.
Find the best prices for regular season and playoff tickets for your favorite teams.
Use your Android Wear to view/scan tickets for eligible games.
Best prices 100% guaranteed.

CONNECT:
Website - rukkus.com
Facebook - facebook.com/jointherukkus
Twitter - twitter.com/jointherukkus
Email hi@rukkus.com

MORE INFO:
Rukkus is a ticket search engine that scans the entire web and puts all the available tickets in one place. Our interactive seat maps and 1st-person seat views make it easy and fun to select your seats. The Rukkus Android app is free with no additional fees. Use Rukkus to find the best deals on concert tickets, sports tickets, theater tickets, and more!
