Description

Hello Expense is a simple, intuitive, and minimalistic app that allows you to enter expenses in a few taps. It provides informative charts and graphs to allow you to analyze the data. Supported features include:

Expenses/income
Customizable categories, currencies, and tags.
Multi-currency and mileage
View by category, day, week, or month
Recurring expenses
Category pie chart
Line graphs
Spreadsheet and QIF export
Backup/restore
