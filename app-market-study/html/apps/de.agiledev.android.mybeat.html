Description

MyBeat FIT is your perfect training partner and keeps track of your heartbeat. Measure your heart rate with bluetooth or ANT+ heart rate belts and record it while you train. Get the most out of your trainings by using training programs which fit to your training state. Improve your overall fitness by following customized trainings.
Detailed analysis of your training help you get better and succeed.
Get ready to train like a champ!
No matter if you want to burn fat or improve your anaerobic threshold. You will find MyBeat FIT offering you the perfect training, e.g.:

- Base Fitness for fat burning and improving your endurance
- Aerobic for improving your endurance and hyperbolic metabolism
- Anaerobic for tempo training and improving your anaerobic threshold
- Interval making you faster than Flash Gordan


Connectivity
Connect your ANT+ or Bluetooth heart rate belt to MyBeat FIT. We support the following heart rate belts:

- Zyphyr
- Polar Wearlink
- all ANT+ belts


Trainings
Choose one of the prebuild trainings, create your own training programs or just train and track your heartbeat.


Analysis
Keep your trainings and see how you get better day by day. Analyse your progress and modify your training to get the best out of you.


Share
Share your success with your friends and use Google FIT for your benefit.


Use awesome features like

All data on one sight
Get all data out of your training (current & average heartrate, percentage from hmax, burned calories).


Training observer
MyBeat observes your training and notifies you when you leave your training zone.


Animated ECG
Visualize your heartbeat by an animated ECG simulation.
