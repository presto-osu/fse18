Description

Let your watchface look like a real watch

Omitsis presents the most carefully designed watch face for Android Wear. Eixample is the beginning of a series of watch faces created by T. Hugo, a professional watch designer based in Berlin, Germany.

Eixample [amp]:
Inspired by this stunning quarter of Barcelona with its wide avenues, its famous grid of apartment blocks and its cosmopolitan way of life.
This is the extraction of one of the most intense urban places in Europe.

Tags: smartwatch, Face, wear, androidwear, watchface, watch face, smartwatchface, watch, blue, luxury, elegant

Compatible watches: LG G Watch, Samsung Gear Live, Motorola 360 (Moto 360).
