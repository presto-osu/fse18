Description

Falling Mosaic is an animated mosaic art watchface.
Unleash the artist in you and design your own mosaic pieces that will form your digital watch face.
Digits are formed from falling mozaic tiles with smooth animations.
It is highly customizable, you can customize the look, size, style, mozaic pieces, colors, animations etc..

It is configurable from the android wear companion app on your phone.
The configuration app provides a live preview of the watch face before sending the changes to your watch.

Features:
Show/Hide date
Date format (new)
24 hours format
Customizable battery indicator (new)
Hide leading zero (new)
Digits Font
Font Size
Background color
Pieces shape (round, square, plus, triangle, etc..)
Pieces colors (auto or custom)
Auto generate colors with configurable color harmony
Seconds indicator piece
Piece drops animation (elastic, bounce, swing, etc..)
Animation frequency (new)
Ambient mode support
Ambient mode preview in app
Live preview for both round and square watches

All android wear watches are supported, round & square:
Moto 360
LG G Watch R
LG G Watch
Samsung Gear Live
Sony Smartwatch 3
Asus ZenWatch
LG Watch Urbane

To install the watch face:
1. After installation wait a couple of minutes so it syncs with your watch
2. Long press your android wear watch and choose Falling Mosaic as your watch face, or select the watch face using the Android Wear app. If you still can't see the watch face, open the android wear application on your phone and run 'Resync app', you should then see it appear.

Please send any issues, feedback, requests to support@samham.net
