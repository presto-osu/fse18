Description

Next Bus Dublin uses the Dublin Bus realtime information system to tell you exactly when your bus is arriving. No more guessing using fictional timetables! The app is packed with useful features, looks great and is easy to use.

Features:

- Easily add, remove and re-order favourite stops to get your times as fast as possible.
- Filter results to see only the routes you want at busy stops.
- Bus alerts so you know exactly when to leave to catch your bus.
- Route map previews so you can check out the route before catching the bus
- Each bus stop page has it's own StreetView so you'll know you're at the right one!
- News updates from Dublin Bus
- Ability to export your favourites so you don't lose them when moving to a new phone.
- Get bus times on your watch with Android Wear support!

"A great replacement to the official Dublin Bus app" - Quinton O'Reilly, Tech Writer, TheJournal.ie

Artwork courtesy of Stuart Scargill

Data provided by Dublin Bus and Biznet. This app is unofficial and not associated with Dublin Bus.
