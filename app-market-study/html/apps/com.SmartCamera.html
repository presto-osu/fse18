Description

Would you like to take a high quality photo or have a photo-shoot with your friends?
Surprise them with this new application - smart HD camera!
smart camera make a series of fascinating pictures and share them with friends. Smart camera will edit the photos taken, add filters to your images and make them more diverse.

The app support Android wear now!

Excellent photo and video effects, it has never been so easy to get HD quality pictures and video. Smart HD camera to take full advantage of your phone or tablet. Now you can capture moments in a fast and easy way.

With this camera app, you can create beautiful new pictures, edit and conveniently upload them to Facebook, Instagram, Picasa anytime, and another set in your application.
Smart HD camera is a free application that features:
Universal Camera , android photo gallery, photo editor, photo uploader to Facebook, Instagram, Gmail, Picassa and any installed application.
Smart Camera features:
Android photos
Quickly take pictures and video
Quick Snap
Optical / Digital zoom
Focus mode (auto, infinity, macro, manual, etc.)
AutoFocus
Touch and Focus
Gallery for viewing and editing images
Upload photos to Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, Google, Twitter or another application
Advanced camera features:
The "big button" for taking selfies
Flash settings (Auto, Touch, Red eyes, Disable, etc.)
White balance (Auto, Incandescent, Daylight, Cloudy, etc.)
Adjusting the exposure
Various scene mode with a variety of photo
Scene Mode (Auto, Action, portrait, landscape, night, snow, etc.)
Features a photo editor:
Photo Effects and Photo Filters: vanilla, pastel, neon, space and photo effects.
Supports Android from 4.0 to 6.0 version
Tested on Samsung Galaxy Ace, Samsung Galaxy S3 Samsung Galaxy S3 Mini, Sony Xperia Arc, Sony Xperia J, Samsung Galaxy Y, Samsung Galaxy Ace 2 , Samsung Galaxy S, HTC One X, HTC Sense, HTC Desire HD, Nexus 7, Sony Ericsson Xperia Arc S, Sony Ericsson Xperia S, Sony Xperia U and works well on other phones Android.
