Description

With Wear Camera you can easily control the camera of your phone using your wearable.

Wear Camera works even if your phone's screen is off or locked. Both Bluetooth and Wi-Fi connections are supported.

You can also switch between front and back camera or change the flash mode.

Don't you have an Android Wear? No problem, you can use Wear Camera as simple camera app, it is super fast!
