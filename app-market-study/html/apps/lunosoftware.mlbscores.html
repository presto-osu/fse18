Description

Get LIVE, FAST scores, stats and alerts for the MLB on your phone, tablet and our new Android Wear app!

All your received alerts are saved in your alert history in case you missed any.

Get standard game alerts and player alerts you won't find anywhere else, all easily configurable.

We use real push technology with no background services running for faster alerts with no wasted bandwidth and battery life.

We also provide:
live scores widget, updated every minute
custom notification cards for Android Wear
live play by play
full team rosters
team schedules
season stats for every player
team news from multiple outlets
post-game highlights and other YouTube videos

The following alert types are available:

game start
game end
score change
lead change
extra inning game
no hitter through 7
no hitter
1 hit from cycle
hit for cycle
4 hits
3 homeruns
4 RBI
5 RBI
3 stolen bases
12 strikeouts
close game (within 2 runs or tying run on deck in 9th)
starting lineups posted

** note: This app is not affiliated with or endorsed by Major League Baseball. Any trademarks used in the app are done so under "fair use" with the sole purpose of identifying the respective entities, and remain the property of their respective owners.
