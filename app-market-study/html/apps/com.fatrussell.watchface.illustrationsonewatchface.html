Description

Illustration Watch Faces by VA

Choose from five watch faces for Android Wear: "Octopus", "Owl King", "Skull", "Elephant" and "Giraffe", each with unique customizations. Change colors, tick marks and more using the Android Wear app!

Add some fun illustrations to your Android Wear watch! All designs created by VA work on circular and square watches.

Octopus: Choose from set colors to control the octopus illustration, background and accents.

Owl King: Custom pick the owl king illustration and background color.

Skull: Custom pick the background color, choose black or white for the skull illustration and choose tick mark style.

Elephant: Choose from set colors for the elephant illustration, accents and background.

Giraffe: Choose from set colors to control the giraffe illustration, background and accents.

12 and 24 hour options included with the "Owl King", "Elephant" and "Giraffe" designs.

To access the customization settings:
Open the Android Wear app (on the phone or tablet paired with your Wear device)
Select your VA Illustration watch face
After selected, click the gear icon over the watch face preview
Customize your watch face!

Add some fun and unique imagery to your Android Wear watch face!
