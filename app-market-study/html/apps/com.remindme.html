Description

Remind Me is a simple yet useful reminder app for your daily tasks which you forget to do & for which your mobile can easily help. Have a user friendly, fast & intuitive User interface for user to manage everything with just the thumb holding the phone with 1 hand. The layout is careful crafted for user to set reminders with ease.

Features of the app include:
- Timely Managed & sorted Listview
- One finger operation for everything
- Color Themes
- Unique UI/UX Design
- Easy category selection by sliding down
- Android Wear Support and Notifications.
- Catch missed calls and offer to call or add reminder.

The app can used as a digital companion which simply reminds you of tasks which you can do with your phone & want to do, But busy schedule does not let do them on time. Its more than a To Do list & Even more than a To Do list with reminders. It helps you by opening the related app on your phone so you just confirm the action & do it.

It helps you to add reminders for:
- Calling a contact
- Sending Text Message
- Emailing a contact
- Add Reminders to Wake up
- Add To Do tasks with or without Reminders
- Add Calender Events for Birthdays & Anniversaries

This version of the app is here with some basic but useful features and more advanced features are still being coded. Features like
- Custom app selection
- Cloud Sync with Chrome app
- Voice Command for Reminders

We at DesignersX sincerely hope this app will help you solve some of the daily problems by reminding you to do tasks. We have tested this app in a closed group for 3 months and improved it as per best of our knowledge. If you still find a bug with your specific phone, we would encourage you to submit crash/error report to App store or email us at support.remindme@designersx.com
