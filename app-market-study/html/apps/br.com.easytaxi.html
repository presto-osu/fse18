Description

Now with a totally revamped interface and a seamless service selection experience. Because you deserve a choice.
Find out why more than 25 million users in 420 cities trust Easy. Enjoy the ride!

Features
- Fast: We always assign the nearest car to pick you up.
- Safe: We thoroughly train our partners. Rating system helps select best drivers.
- Efficient: Book a regular taxi or arrive in style with EasyPremium. Save up to 40% by sharing the ride with others.*

* The availability of services vary per location.

How to use
- Wait for the app to automatically locate your address using GPS.
- Confirm your location. We suggest you add your credit card to unlock extra features.
- Tap Request here" and watch how Easy assigns a nearby driver. Follow him on the map and in minutes hell be at the location you requested.
