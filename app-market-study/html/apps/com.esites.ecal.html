Description

Featured in VentureBeat, Forbes, Sony and iCulture.

WAVE Calendar is a free calendar app with a beautiful UI and a smooth navigation. The app includes free tasks and additional calendars to enrich your calendar. To make your calendar experience even better you can download three widgets, add the local weather, add attachments, and add special tags to appointments. Besides this, the app includes all the standard features you would expect from a calendar app!

Also available for iPhone 6, 6+ and wearables.

Check out our social media pages for the latest updates and news!
Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/WAVEcalendar
Twitter: @WAVECalendarapp

Do you have a question or feedback for us? Email us at support@wavecalendarapp.com.
