Description

Official app brought to you by Conotoxia - online currency exchange office.

Discover our global brand! Cinkciarz is available in Polish. Other language versions are presented as Conotoxia.
Two brands, the same services.

Following features:

Exchange rates:
- Conotoxia exchange rates
- NBP exchange rates
- Currency converter
- Exchange rates widget (widgets manually refresh every 15 sec),

Transactions from bank account:
- Buy / sell / exchange
- Operations history

Transactions from currency wallet:
- Buy / sell / exchange
- Summary
- Top up
- Withdraw
- History of operations
- History of blockages

Social transactions:
- Buy / sell
- Your orders
- Top 20

Your profile:
- Banking accounts
- Recipients
- Messages
- Your data
- Change password
- Discount codes
- Settings

Services:
- Standing orders
- Direct payments
- Currency alerts
- Currency cards
- ATM withdrawal
- SMS exchange rates
- Text messages packages

Other:
- Create account
- Branches
- Consultants
- News
- Podcasts and Vodcasts
- Podcast Player for Android Auto
- Language settings - the app supports 35 languages (Polish, English (US), English (UK), French, Italian, Spanish, Portuguese (Brazil), Portuguese (Portugal), Catalan, German, Dutch, Danish, Swedish, Finnish, Norwegian, Russian, Czech, Slovak, Croatian, Romanian, Turkish, Ukrainian, Hungarian, Traditional Chinese, Simplified Chinese, Korean, Japanese, Vietnamese, Arabic, Thai, Greek, Hebrew, Hindi, Indonesian, Malay),

Attention! In case of any issues concerning the application, please attach a detailed description of the problem via Google Play (select "Email Developer" in the "Contact Developer" section).
