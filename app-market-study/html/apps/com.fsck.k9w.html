Description

WristMail is a fully fledged mail client for Android and Android Wear with:

- Full Android Wear email notifications support, including stacked notifications, full second page preview and actions
- The keyboards from Wear Messenger.
- A full app for the watch, letting you see your email inbox and reply

More features will come with time and as requests come. Sure, I meant requests! I will always glad to listen your ideas to improve this app, so please if you have any let me know!
Please remember, this is the first version. There may be imperfections, but we promise we will try to figure them out as soon as possible.

Please check the faq (the website link below) if you have any questions. If you can't find an answer, feel free to contact me.
