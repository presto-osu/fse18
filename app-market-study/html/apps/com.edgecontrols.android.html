Description

A simple tool for controlling the brightness of the wear device - sliding your finger along some of the edges of the screen will change your brightness.
Long press on the edge is switching on the brightness to Auto Mode.
Best for wearable devices with no integrated sensor for light.

Compatible with round wearable devices.

COMING - you'll be able to control the sound of the phone the same way!
