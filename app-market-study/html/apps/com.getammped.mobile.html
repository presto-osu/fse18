Description

AMP Your Workout introduces the world's most significant advancement in weight training for over 50 years via its AMMP system, a patented training guidance system that monitors ALL training data relevant for physique development and guides you to reach your peak and maximise muscle stimulus for each exercise.

The more you train, the more the system is able to analyze your performance characteristics and predict your optimum workout target. You get fast results because you train the way your body is meant to train

Make the most of your time in the gym.

AMP Your Workout is an easy to use app that, via complex technology, measures every aspect of your training and learns how your body works with each workout you perform. AMP Your Workout tracks ALL the parameters important for results, including your rest periods between sets and even HOW you perform each exercise. It then computes the benefit of each exercise and will predict and prepare your next training session according to the targeted improvement that you set. With each workout the system gains more knowledge of your optimum training and you will know you are training to your full potential to speed up progress towards your goals.

AMP Your Workout, via your training data, identifies your strengths and weaknesses and can then better guide you for fast results. Training experts continually stress the importance of not training like your friends or favourite sports star, but instead training in a way to suit your genetics. However, up till now, there has never been a system to gather and analyse all the relevant data and importantly, to then guide you for continual improvement.

AMP Your Workout uses smart phones and watches to gather data about your training.

Whilst a compatible smart-watch (see website) is the best solution, AMP Your Workout offers other options -
Use the accelerometer in your phone (requires a means of wearing the phone on your arm)
train without an accelerometer provided you train in strict 1010 fashion (see the video for more details)

Using wearable technology, you can train in any one of hundreds of tempo combinations and AMP Your Workout will monitor every part of every rep and compute the benefit of each rep on the muscles you are targeting combined with your Lifting style the system will compute the true value of the set.

The heart of the AMP Your Workout system is our array of sophisticated algorithms and predictive analysis algorithms that use your existing database to understand your ability with each exercise, and precisely predict your capabilities in order to optimise the loads you place on your muscles to create the results you want.

The system uses Black-Box methodology to understand and model a complex system - you!

Our Black Box methodology, will compute the many variables that go to make up your muscle performance, overload, and recovery, will ensure that you can optimise all aspects of your training for fast results.

Try it - you will be amazed at how it can predict your ultimate workout and push you to your goals more effectively and in ways you never dreamed of!

Suitable for beginner trainers right through to elite and professional athletes.

Thanks for helping us out!
