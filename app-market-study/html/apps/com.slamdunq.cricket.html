Description

Slamdunq cricket is a one of a kind app aimed to help you improve your bowling skills.

Our carefully designed matrices such as run up speed, arm speed, angle of deviation, wrist rotation and release height will up your bowling techniques whether its a pace bowler or spin bowler. A real-time feedback, bowling tips and 3D animation of your bowling action bring cricket coaching right to your wrist / phone.

So strap on your watch, power our app and bring down those wickets

Supported devices:
1. Samsung gear S2
2. LG-G and LG-Urbane watches
