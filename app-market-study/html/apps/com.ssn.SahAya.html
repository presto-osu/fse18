Description

**** BEST SAFETY APP ******

**** BEST WOMEN SAFETY APP ******

**** WORKS ANYWHERE IN THE WORLD ****

**** FREE App - NO ads ****

**** Trigger alert using Android Wear ****

**** SahAya is linked to POLICE DEPARTMENT IN BANGALORE, INDIA - IF YOU ARE IN BANGALORE, INDIA DOWNLOAD BCP SahAya - http://goo.gl/t4FNMg ****

KEY FEATURES:

** No internet (mobile data) required.
** Trigger help by pressing Power Button or through Android Wear Watch
** Works on any android mobile device
** Can continuously send alerts to your contacts for a given time
** Password protected contacts
** Weekly reminder
** Add ten phone numbers of your family/relatives/friends
** Add ten email address of your family/relatives/friends
** Post alerts on Facebook wall.

When there's an emergency situation that poses an immediate risk to health, life, property or environment, do you think you have time to search for contacts and try to call them? In case that person is not available, do you look for another contact person and reach out for them? Have you ever thought calling people for help would be as easy as just a click of a button?

Here's an app which can alert multiple people about your whereabouts with just a click of a button! Here's how it can be done.

1. Press the phone power button more than 5 times to request help.

or

1. Click on the HELP button
2. Zoom!!! Your help is already on its way!!!

App sends out an alert message (through text messaging, email and posts status alert on Facebook) that you need help, sends your location information, IMEI number of your phone, SIM number and also sends a link of google maps showing them the route to reach you.

Are you now wondering whom it may send the information to? Yes, there's a provision in this app to store 10 contacts of your near and dear ones that you would want to inform about your emergency situation.

Other important features:

1. SahAya can also send 'I'm Safe now' message to your contacts.
2. SahAya has an option change the number of times the power button has to be pressed

Dont forget to add SahAya widget for quick access.

Disclaimer:

1. SahAya works with network reception (for sms) and internet connectivity (for email and Facebook).

2. SahAya works well on a single SIM card phone or Dual SIM card phone with primary SIM Card slot active.

3. Please note SahAya can only send text message (SMS) of your whereabouts and doesn't make any emergency calls.

4. SahAya works well when Location Services is ON always!
