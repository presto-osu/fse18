Description

This is the first CPU Benchmark App for Android Wear?
If you want to know how fast the Processor of your phone or of your Android Wear Watch ist, this is the right App for you!
CPU Benchmark simply calculates a ton of additions, and in relation to the time the processor needs for that operation, your phone/watch will be rated with specific points.
Due to some reasons, the points will vary a bit. To get a distinguished result it is necessary to run about 5-10 test and look at the average points.
Have fun with CPU Benchmark!
