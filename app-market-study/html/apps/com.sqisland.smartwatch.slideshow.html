Description

Show pictures on your SmartWatch as a slideshow.

This application collects all the images on your phone and display them on your watch. You can select a subset of photos by picking a specific folder. Swipe left and right during the slideshow to move images back and forth.

Take your watch off the strap and clip it everywhere to show off your photos! Wear it as a name tag, and be the star at your next party.

Note: This application will not be shown in your phone application screen. Access application settings via LiveWare manager, where you can also enable or disable this SmartWatch application.
