Description

Antix is the best video editing app for creating awesome shareable videos. Antix works with your Android smartphone and GoPro camera. Perfect for quickly creating videos of your action sports, holidays, adventures and all those amazing moments you capture. No in-app purchases - Antix is completely free!

VIDEO EDITING FEATURES

+ Edit videos from your gallery or capture from the app
+ Control and browse your GoPro camera to download clips straight into the video editor
+ Trim, flip and re-arrange video clips
+ Import multiple clips in one go
+ Add slow motion and colour enhancing filters
+ Add music tracks from our free collection
+ Volume equaliser for perfect sound balancing
+ Preview your video while you edit
+ Publish videos to your Antix profile and save to your camera roll
+ Instantly share to Facebook, Twitter and post to Instagram

GOPRO COMPATIBLE

- Connect and control your GoPro with WiFi
- Ride with Antix, and your phone will track your motion to identify all the awesome moments and download them straight to your phone
- Browse and select your favourite video clips from your GoPro and Antix will download them straight the Antix movie editor
- Works with GoPro Hero4, Hero3+, Hero3, Hero+ LCD, Hero4 Session*

DISCOVER

- Discover incredible videos on our explore page
- Like, comment and share your favourite videos from our community

Were completely ad free!

Antix is an global community of action and extreme sports people. Watch awesome snowboard, ski, skateboard, MTB, BMX scooter, adventure and travel videos on our trending feed. Upload your video and get discovered!

Every day we feature a new Antix video on our FB, Instagram, Twitter and YouTube profiles...if youd like to get featured get creating with Antix now.
