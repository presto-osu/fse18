Description

*** THERE IS NO MALWARE IN WEAR MESSENGER. IF YOUR ANTIVIRUS SAYS THERE IS, IT'S A FALSE POSITIVE (please, get a better antivirus!) ***


Wear Messenger is an app which greatly expands your ways to send SMS, Gmail, Hangouts, WhatsApp and Facebook Messenger messages with your fingers.


THIS IS A DEMO VERSION.


The purpose of this version is to let you evaluate if Wear Messenger is worth your money or not. It contains several limitations, such as:
- No notifications
- Notification linking not available
- Only one conversation visible
- Only two messages per conversation
- Only five contacts available
- No xposed module

However, we left all the settings to let you see what you can actually change/have in the full version.
