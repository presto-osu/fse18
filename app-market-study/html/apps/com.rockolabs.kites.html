Description

Discover the world through friends with Kites. Follow your friends' experiences so you can enjoy what they did, avoid what they didn't, try what they recommend and so much more. Even if you end up with a couple of hours free in your home town, you'll be surprised what you can discover from the comfort of your palm!

Kites is all about discovering and sharing. Share your thoughts about places you visit; restaurants, parks, bars, tours, plays, whatever! You can even leave kites privately for specific friends - which means you can even share things like personal memories and love notes! The more you share, the more knowledge you give your followers!

And you can do so much more:
Browse publicly to see kites left by anyone, anywhere in the world
Group kites into collections and show your friends things like 'The best places in Paris for a romantic stroll' or 'The best places in London to do nothing!'
Star the kites you enjoy most, and Kites will start to learn what kind of experiences you enjoy, so it can become more relevant to you
Collect stars to receive commendations and beat your friends!
Set up alerts, by pinning kites, to remind you about places when you are nearby
And remember, Kites works best with friends, so get them on board to increase your network and unlock great discoveries around the globe!

Download Kites now and start leaving messages where they matter!

Don't forget to rate and +1 Kites above!
We love to hear from you - contact us at hello@kitesapp.com
Like Kites on Facebook - https://facebook.com/kitesapp
Follow Kites on Twitter - https://twitter.com/kitesapp

Here's a quick run down of why Kites needs the permissions it asks for:
Identity: This lets us see the email addresses on your device so we make creating a new account one step shorter, and also lets you log in with Google Plus
Contacts / Calendar: To see which of your contacts use Kites (your contact list will NEVER be uploaded to our servers). We don't use your calendar, but Android lumps these together (I know right..)
Location: Kites wouldn't really work without being able to tell where you are
SMS: To let you tell your friends about Kites (we only send an sms when you ask us to)
Photos / Media / Files: So you can set your profile picture from an image in your gallery
Camera / Microphone: So you can take your profile picture directly. Again, we don't use your microphone, Android just puts them together
Device ID & call information: This lets use see more details about your device, such as its make and model, so we can see which devices use Kites the most and optimise the experience. We don't use your call information

Wow, all the way to the bottom! Now download Kites and spread the message to your friends :)
