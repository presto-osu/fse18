Description

According to "The 3-Hours Diet" you should eat every 3 hours. However, many people forget eating meals during the days. Every3 will help you remember when to eat on time.

Eating every 3 hours will boost your body fat burning, prevent low-blood sugar (Hypoglycemia) and help you lose weight.

How does it work? The app is designed to be as simple as possible. All you have to do is download and enter the app once. That's it!

Every3 features: Monitor your meals by marking them as eaten or not, statistics (Month, Days in month, specific day) and a full screen alarm.


So you want a healthier life and monitor your daily meals Every3 is the app for you!


Did you like the app? Have some good ideas how to improve it? Let us know!
